Connect Your Google Sheet

To show live data from your Google Form responses:

  1. Create your Google Form and link it to a Google Sheet
  2. In Google Sheets, go to File → Share → Publish to the web
  3. Choose the response sheet tab, select CSV format, and click Publish
  4. Copy the published CSV URL and paste it below

Expected columns (in order): Timestamp, Church Name, City, State, Languages, Staff Size, then one column per feeling question (values: Yes/No or 1-5 scale)
